Features

There's a simple reason dozens choose WikiForge


WikiForge offers an unprecedented amount of features. With so many features, WikiForge is the sure fit for your wiki.

Custom domains

Others charge you for the ability to use a custom domain. We allow it on all our plans. Simply point the domain to us and we'll do the rest.

CirrusSearch (ElasticSearch), out of the box

Use the powerful search engine that organizations like Wikimedia use in order to shift through mountains of data and text. ElasticSearch provides a seamless in-wiki search experience that the default MediaWiki search engine just can't provide.

VisualEditor's ease at hand

Not a pro at wikitext editing? No problem! All WikiForge plans come with VisualEditor support. Orchestrate magnificent pages, easily, with the unrivaled WYSIWYG abilities of VisualEditor.

LDAP, OpenID, SAML, and other SSO solutions supported

Managing accounts can be a hassle, more so in an enterprise enviornment. Don't get fragmented! All plans come with SSO support so that system administrators can effortlessly take the reins of their users, including support for LDAP, OpenID, SAML, and more.

Private wikis

Collaborate privately and in confidenitality with private wikis. On private wikis, only users you allow can view the wiki's content. Don't expose your secrets on the internet ever again.

Mobile responsive layouts and features

55% of all traffic on the internet comes from phones. Accomodate and contour to them with our mobile-ready responsive layouts, skins, and features. MobileFrontend and Minerva Neue are supported out of the box to provide an smooth mobile experience.

Semantic MediaWiki supported

Enjoy the feature rich Semantic MediaWiki? So do we! WikiForge offers Semantic MediaWiki support and support for some of its plugins.

Hundreds of extensions, at your fingertips

No one rivals WikiForge's library! With over 300 extensions and skins, WikiForge has extensions sure to satisfy and exceed your requirements.

Let professionals do the heavy lifting

Spend less time worrying about server maintenance and upgrades and spend more time relaxing! We handle everything for you and in the background so that you can spend more time focusing on your wiki and organization.
